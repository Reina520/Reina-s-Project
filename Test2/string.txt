Players explore vast, open-world environments filled with quests and hidden treasures.

Combat mechanics in many games allow players to use a variety of weapons and abilities.

Players can craft new items and weapons using resources found in the game world.

Many games feature multiplayer modes where players can cooperate or compete against each other.

Players must level up their characters to unlock new skills and abilities.

Some games use physics engines to simulate realistic movement and interactions.

The survival genre challenges players to manage hunger, health, and resources in a harsh environment.

In city-building games, players design and manage their own cities, balancing resources and happiness.
